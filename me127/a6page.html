Risks with Design:

Claw is heavy and adds mass to the robot, increasing center of gravity. This will hurt its ability to climb the ramp.

The first design I made had the 180 degree servo at the end of the arm. This added weight far away from the 360 servo and added a larger torque, so I moved it back. I actually think that this is adding more weight because of the second arm that needs to exist and I'm going to remove it.

First improvement, the robot is too large to fit into the 12 in cube, I will raise the mounting point of the claw which will allow for a shorter arm that should fit into the box.

Second improvement, the arm seems to struggle sometimes to rotate, I think a shorter arm will help this but also a counterweight to decrease the torque that needs to be applied. Will have to check about allowed materials, was thinking coins for counterweight.
